Indoor Air Quality and Ductwork Services Los Angeles

Indoor air quality is more important in Southern California than in most US markets for three specific reasons: extended wildfire season pulls smoke into homes through normal HVAC operation, coastal humidity creates conditions for mold and mildew growth, and modern energy-efficient sealed homes trap pollutants that older drafty homes vented naturally.
