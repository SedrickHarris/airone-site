What are the HVAC demands in Hawthorne?

Hawthorne sits in the South Bay inland-adjacent area with marine influence moderating summer heat. Summer temperatures typically reach 82 to 92 degrees Fahrenheit producing a long moderate cooling season. Mild winters with limited heating demand. The dense residential and commercial mix produces consistent year-round HVAC service demand. LAX proximity introduces some particulate air quality considerations for nearby neighborhoods.

What special HVAC considerations apply to Hawthorne homes?

Proximity to LAX introduces aircraft-related particulate that can affect indoor air quality in some Hawthorne neighborhoods. MERV 13 filtration captures the fine particulate produced by aviation emissions more effectively than standard MERV 8 filters.
