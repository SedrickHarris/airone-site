Los Angeles County HVAC Service Area - 82 Cities

AIRONE provides residential and commercial HVAC service across all 82 named cities in Los Angeles County - from the Antelope Valley high desert to the South Bay coast, the San Fernando Valley to the San Gabriel Valley, and Beverly Hills to Bell Gardens.

According to NOAA historical temperature data, San Fernando Valley cities - Burbank, Glendale, Pasadena, West Covina, Calabasas, Santa Clarita - regularly exceed 105°F in July and August. AIRONE recommends marine-grade coil coatings on all condensers installed within 1 to 2 miles of the ocean.

Antelope Valley microclimate. Lancaster and Palmdale operate in a meaningfully different climate from the LA basin - colder winters and hotter summers. AIRONE's Manual J inputs use TMY weather data specific to the Antelope Valley high desert, not generic LA basin data, so your system is sized for the conditions your home actually experiences.
