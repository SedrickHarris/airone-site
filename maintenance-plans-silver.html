What's Included

What Does the Silver Maintenance Plan Include?

The Silver Plan covers both HVAC systems in your home - the air conditioner and the furnace or heating system - with one professional visit per system per year. Every visit includes a complete inspection, cleaning, and performance verification. No 20-minute visual checks. Every AIRONE maintenance visit ends with a written report documenting the system's condition, any components flagged for monitoring, and any repairs completed.

Annual AC Tune-Up - Included Every Year

  • Capacitor microfarad reading tested against nameplate rating - degraded capacitors flagged for replacement before they cause a no-start failure
  • Contactor condition inspected - pitted or worn contacts replaced before failure
  • System operating pressures measured and compared against manufacturer specifications for current outdoor temperature
  • Refrigerant charge level verified - slow leak detection if readings are off
  • Compressor, condenser fan motor, and blower motor amperage draws measured against nameplate ratings
  • Evaporator and condenser coils inspected and cleaned
  • Air filter inspected - replacement recommended with flat-rate pricing if needed
  • Supply and return temperature differential measured
  • Condensate drain line flushed and float switch tested
  • All electrical connections tightened and inspected for corrosion
  • Written maintenance report provided at end of visit

Annual Furnace Inspection - Included Every Year

  • Heat exchanger inspected for cracks or carbon deposits - a cracked heat exchanger is a safety issue requiring immediate attention
  • Burner assembly cleaned and flame pattern inspected
  • Ignition system tested - hot surface igniter or spark igniter confirmed operational
  • Flue and venting pathway inspected for blockage, corrosion, or improper slope
  • Gas pressure measured and verified at manifold and inlet
  • Blower motor amperage draw and wheel condition inspected
  • Heat rise measured across the heat exchanger and compared against manufacturer specification
  • Thermostat calibration checked and all wiring connections inspected
  • Written maintenance report provided at end of visit

Priority Scheduling - Members Scheduled First

  • Silver Plan members receive priority scheduling over non-member service calls year-round
  • Priority scheduling applies to both annual tune-up visits and any repair calls that arise between scheduled visits
  • During peak summer and winter periods, priority scheduling provides access to earlier appointment windows when non-member availability may be limited
  • Appointment windows available Monday through Saturday, 7 AM to 7 PM

Written Maintenance Report - After Every Visit

  • Full written condition report provided to the homeowner at the end of every visit
  • Documents all work performed, all components inspected, and all readings taken during the visit
  • Flags any components showing early degradation with a clear explanation of what was found and what the risk is if left unaddressed
  • Provides a documented service record that supports manufacturer warranty compliance - most major HVAC brands require annual professional maintenance to keep equipment warranties valid
  • Useful for home sale disclosure, insurance documentation, and warranty claim support

10 Percent Member Discount on Parts and Repair Labor

  • 10 percent member discount applies to all parts and repair labor on any service call during the plan year - not just the scheduled maintenance visits
  • Discount applies whether the repair is identified during the tune-up or results from a separate service call
  • No cap on the number of repair calls eligible for the member discount
  • Discount is applied automatically as a Silver Plan member - no coupon or code required
What to Expect

What Happens on a Silver Plan Service Visit?

Here is exactly what to expect when an AIRONE technician arrives for a Silver Plan maintenance visit - from scheduling to the written report you receive at the end.

1

Step 1 - Schedule Your Visit (5 Minutes)

Call (323) 471-1037 or use the form on this page to schedule your annual AC tune-up or furnace inspection. Silver Plan members receive priority scheduling - mention your plan membership when you call and you will be booked ahead of non-member calls. AIRONE schedules AC tune-ups in advance of the summer cooling season (ideally March through May) and furnace inspections in advance of the heating season (ideally September through November).

2

Step 2 - Technician Arrival and System Walk-Through (10 Minutes)

Your AIRONE technician arrives in the confirmed appointment window, reviews the system type and service history with you, and does a brief visual walk-through before beginning work. If you have noticed any performance issues - unusual sounds, higher energy bills, uneven cooling or heating - mention them at this stage so the technician knows where to pay additional attention.

3

Step 3 - Full System Inspection and Service (45 to 75 Minutes)

The technician works through the complete inspection and service checklist for your system - electrical components, refrigerant system, mechanical components, coils, airflow, and safety controls for the AC visit; heat exchanger, burner, ignition, venting, gas pressure, and airflow for the furnace visit. Every reading is recorded on the maintenance report in real time. The visit takes approximately 45 to 75 minutes depending on system type and condition.

4

Step 4 - Findings Review and Flat-Rate Repair Options (10 Minutes)

If the technician identifies any components that need attention, they review the findings with you directly - showing you the diagnostic reading where applicable - and provide a flat-rate repair quote before any additional work is performed. You have three options: approve the repair on the same visit, schedule a separate repair appointment, or decline the repair. No work is performed without your explicit approval. If nothing needs repair, the technician tells you that clearly and it is documented in writing.

5

Step 5 - Written Maintenance Report Left With You

Before leaving, your technician provides the written maintenance report documenting all work performed, all readings taken, all components inspected, and any follow-up recommendations. Keep this report - it is your documented service record for manufacturer warranty compliance, home sale disclosure, and insurance purposes. AIRONE can provide copies of prior visit reports on request.

Is Silver Right for You?

Who Is the Silver Maintenance Plan Best For?

The Silver Plan is designed for homeowners who want reliable annual coverage for both their AC and furnace without the additional visits and features of the Gold tier.

Homeowners with a newer HVAC system (under 8 years old)

Newer systems in good mechanical condition benefit from annual preventive maintenance to maintain manufacturer warranty compliance and catch the first signs of component wear before they become repair calls. One visit per system per year is appropriate coverage for a well-maintained newer system in the 1 to 7 year range.

Homeowners who want a documented service record without bi-annual visits

Both Silver and Gold plan members receive a written maintenance report after every visit. For homeowners preparing for a home sale, managing an insurance claim, or maintaining manufacturer warranty documentation, the Silver Plan provides a clean annual service record with the same report quality as the Gold tier.

Homeowners who want priority scheduling and repair discounts without a higher tier

Priority scheduling and the 10 percent member discount on parts and repair labor apply to every Silver Plan member throughout the plan year - not just on scheduled visit days. If a repair need arises between tune-up visits, Silver Plan members receive priority scheduling and the member discount on that repair call automatically.
